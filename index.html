Accomplished

Community Raises 100 Solana To Restart

The previous owner took all funds and left the 2000+ holders with $0. The foundation of Bored Ape Solana Club restarted because the community came together and raised a total of 100 Solana (which at the time 1 Solana equaled $100). Roughly 60 people donated and were compensated with a FREE BASC NFT once the project was relaunched.

DeRug 6001 BASC NFT's Within 5 Days

Within a week 5000+ NFT's were airdropped back to BASC holders and team leaders answered roughly 1000 community support tickets to send another 500 NFT's 1 by 1.

Relist on Secondary Marketplaces: Magic Eden, OpenSea, WeYu, Solanart

Bored Ape Solana Club can now be found on the top 4 marketplaces for Solana NFT’s. In order to buy and sell NFT’s a collection must be listed on a marketplace.

Update Bored Ape Solana Club Artwork and Metadata

All 6001 BASC NFT trait combinations are unique and do not match any Ethereum BAYC NFT. Inhouse artist, Kitsune, has hand drawn all traits while providing some Solana modifications and adjusting the base layer character details.

Create Real World Impacts

Everyday of the week, BASC hosts an event where active members inside discord can participate and win rewards such as: FREE pizza, gas, movie tickets, sushi, etc. This allows investors to receive an instant, tangible gift for investing into their BASC NFT.

Speak At FX Summit 2022 Conference

Dak Daze, community leader, was invited to speak on stage at The FX Summit (a 3 day crypto, forex, nft convention in Miami, Florida). Hosted by Chart Addicts, it's first year held over 1000 people in attendance. Dak Daze will attend the FX Summit 2023.

Build New Bored Ape Solana Club Website

The community requested a brand new website to be made! The task was onboarded by in house web designer, James Kobe for a payment of 1 BASC NFT. James has now joined the team as the full time web developer for any future updates.

Introduce BASC Care Packages

The community had always been promised a royalty payout from the previous project owner that unfortunately never took place. BASC Care Packages are random airdrops of Solana to community members who have their BASC NFT's not listed on a marketplace for sale. The first BASC Care Package was 1 Solana airdropped to 100 BASC NFT's.

Chart Addicts Official Partnership

Bored Ape Solana Club is the official PFP project for Chart Addicts, a premiere coaching service for retail traders worldwide. Owning a BASC NFT gets Chart Addicts members a 25% off discount on their VIP Membership. The traits of Gold Fur & Gold Grill BASC NFT's are good for a Life Time Membership to Chart Addicts.
